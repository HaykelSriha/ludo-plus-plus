## special boxes
- 67 box : shuffles all players randomly except those are in safe boxes (only one in the game)
- mine : kills the player instantly
- prison : the player can't play for 3 turns, players can stack in prison (2 prisons) without eating each other
- atomic bomb : all players die who aren't in safe cases (one in the game)
- gun : kill any player of your choice (2)
- +1 turn : get additional turn (2)
- portals : there re 4 of them and can teleport you to another portal randomly
- heart: let you get out one of your players
